Honey Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Description

An easy to make dish that is serve with rice or quinoa.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

Steps

  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy souce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours.
