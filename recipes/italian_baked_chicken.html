Tender Italian Baked Chicken

Description

Flavorful and moist baked chicken.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. In a bow, mix the mayonnaise, Parmesan chees, and garlic powder. Place bread crumbs in a separate bow. Dip chicken into the mayonnaise mixture, then into the bread crumbs to coat. Arrange coated chicken on a baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven, or until chicken juices run clear and the coathing is golden brown.
